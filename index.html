EARTH DAY 2022

Create your own
DIY residents home

Earth Day 2022 is rapidly approaching and
three residents need your help finding a new
home. Follow this sites simple instructions to
choose your resident, gather materials, and
make them the perfect home!







Choose your Resident

These three residents are on the hunt
for a new home. Read their descriptions
and decide who your home will be
made for. Don’t worry, every
resident will get a home.

Materials

There are a number of
substitutes to each material
needed to create your
residents house.

Sticks/Popcicle Sticks


It will be good to have around 20 sticks depending on how big you want your house to be.

Rocks/ Pebbles


Five rocks or so will be perfect to create your resident’s perfect pathway leading home.

Moss/ Nature Material


If you are able to grab some natural material it looks great on your roof and makes the house cozier.

Optional Extras


Depending on your resident you might think they’ll like something extra you have around, add it in.

Instructions

You can skip or add in as
many steps as you’d like.
There are substitutes to every step

Find your lot


Your resident can live
outside or indoors, it’s
up to you. The best
lots are flatter.

Create your Walls


Using your wooden
sticks, create three
even sized walls &
glue them together to
form an open square.

Create your Roof


Using wooden sticks,
create a roof for your
home. The shape is
up to you.

Create your walkway


Using rocks, create a
pathway so your
resident has a way
to get in.

Decorate


Using chosen materials
decorate your house to
your choosing, think
about what your resident like.

Enjoy!


Wow, your resident
loves their home! You
can update and fix it
as you choose.
